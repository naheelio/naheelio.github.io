Google Arts & Culture AR Prototyping
Based on my AR/VR prototpying work for Classrooms, I was asked to join a team of UX engineers in SF who were consulting with the Google Arts & Culture team.

We worked remotely to concept, prototype, and present ideas to the GA&C team.

We then spent two weeks with the team in Paris and London, fleshing out some of the top concepts alongside their product team and presenting to their VP.

I gathered UX insights from the projects we prototyped to leave with their team as a tool when they went into implementing some of these features into their app.

This partnership had an impact on the GA&C team, and they launched one of the concepts we worked with them on in their app.
