1:1 Coffee
You’ll often find throughout the year that you have trouble bringing your entire team together. These are times when you can use a simple 1:1 coffee to keep up with individual members of your team to hear what is happening on the ground level, and show that you still care to hear from them. Feedback throughout the year is important to maintaining open communication with your team.
Download Materials
Case Study
Steps
1
Take some time to think about the person you are going to have coffee with, and note some positive feedback, as well as constructive criticism if you have any.
2
Start the chat by sharing some of the positive feedback you have for them, and then start to ask them about their experience so far, as well as any pressing issues you would like their thoughts on.
3
End off by thanking them and asking for ways you can support them moving forward. While part of this activity is about understanding perspectives of different members of your team, one of the most powerful outcomes is showing your team that you’re their to support them and that you appreciate the work they have been doing.
