Case Study
Steps
1
Figure out an objective for the activity. This will help you with the prompts you write on the downloadable materials, as well as help you guide the conversation as the activity moves forward.
2
Prepare the downloadable materials, and lay them out alongside some glue and markers.
Hint: Have both a fun prompt, as well as a more serious goal-focussed one. This is really important because the first fun prompt will help the participants feel more comfortable before you transition to a more serious topic.
3
Explain the activity and give them 10 minutes to complete a prompt. Feel free to let them chat or ask questions during this time.
4
Get each participant to explain their collage. As they do this, ask open-ended questions to get them to expand on what they mean, and ask other people their opinions to get a group discussion going. These conversations are where you will uncover real insights.
5
Repeat the process with your next prompt until you are done. We suggest one fun prompt, along with two serious prompts.
